## Account and Security

BusinessAnalysis.io prioritizes your data security. You create accounts using email verification, enable two-factor authentication (2FA), and manage permissions at the team level.

<ExpandableGroup>

<Expandable title="How do I create a BusinessAnalysis.io account?" default-open="true">

Visit the [sign-up page](https://businessanalysis.io/signup) and provide your email, name, and a strong password. Verify your email to activate the account. Teams start with a free plan supporting up to 5 users.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What happens if I forget my password?">

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email to receive a reset link valid for 15 minutes. Change your password immediately and enable 2FA for added protection.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?">

Navigate to Account Settings > Security. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (like Google Authenticator) and enter the code. Back up your recovery codes securely.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I use single sign-on (SSO)?">

Enterprise plans support SSO via Google Workspace, Microsoft Azure AD, or Okta. Contact support to configure SAML 2.0 integration.

</Expandable>

</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">
Store recovery codes offline. Never share your 2FA secrets.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Encounter problems? Follow these steps to resolve most issues quickly.

<Steps>

<Step title="Check your internet connection" icon="wifi">

Test your connection speed at [speedtest.net](https://www.speedtest.net). BusinessAnalysis.io requires a stable connection with `<10ms` latency for real-time collaboration.

</Step>

<Step title="Clear browser cache and cookies">

Open your browser settings:

<Tabs>

<Tab title="Chrome" icon="chrome">

1. Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+Delete` (macOS).  
2. Select "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files".  
3. Clear data and reload the page.

```bash
# Or use DevTools
# F12 > Application > Storage > Clear site data
```

</Tab>

<Tab title="Firefox" icon="firefox">

1. Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete`.  
2. Choose "Cookies" and "Cache".  
3. Clear and restart.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

</Step>

<Step title="Disable browser extensions">

Extensions like ad blockers can interfere. Disable them temporarily via your browser's extensions menu and test again.

</Step>

<Step title="Contact support">

If issues persist, use the in-app help widget or email support@businessanalysis.io with screenshots.

</Step>

</Steps>
