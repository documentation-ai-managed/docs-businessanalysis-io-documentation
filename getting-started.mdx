## Account Creation and Login

Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://businessanalysis.io` and click **Sign Up**.

    Enter your email, create a strong password, and provide your full name.

    Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the homepage and click **Log In**.

    Enter your credentials. Enable two-factor authentication for added security.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    You land on the main dashboard. Bookmark this page for quick access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a business email for account recovery and team invitations.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Task

Start managing your work by adding a task. Choose your preferred method:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Quick Task" icon="plus">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Add Task" icon="plus-circle">
        From the dashboard, select **New Task**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill Details" icon="edit-3">
        Enter title: `Prepare Q1 Financial Report`.

        Set due date: `2024-03-15`.

        Assign to yourself and add description: `Analyze revenue data and create charts.`
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save" icon="check-circle">
        Click **Create**. The task appears in your list.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Advanced Task" icon="settings">
    Use the full form to set priority (`high`), labels (`finance`), and attach files.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Project

Organize related tasks into a project:

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="folder-plus">
    Navigate to **Projects** > **New Project**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure" icon="sliders">
    Name: `Q4 Marketing Campaign`.

    Description: `Launch new product awareness campaign across social channels.`

    Set start date: `2024-10-01` and end date: `2024-12-31`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Milestones" icon="flag">
    Create milestones like `Content Creation` and `Ad Launch`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
